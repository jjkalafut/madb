  Meet the MADB Team

    The MADB Team is comprised of Georgia Tech MS in Analytics students with backgrounds in computer science, data analytics and business consulting.
   We leveraged our diverse industry and technical perspectives to add value throughout the MADB development and analytics pipeline process.

  Roles and Responsibilities

    We separated project roles into the following categories:

Data Preparation



1) Identifying suitable data sets
2) Preparing and transforming data for further analysis
3) Storing data in an efficient and cost-effective manner

Algorithm Selection and Data Analysis

1) Testing various analytics techniques on cleaned data informed by best practices
2) Evaluating model performance according to project objectives
3) Scaling selected model(s) to perform adequately on full data sets

Data Visualization and Project Management

1) Creating interactive representations of final models for presentation and analysis
2) Developing compelling web pages to house and contextualize final models and visualizations
3) Ensuring adherence to deliverable requirements and project timelines
